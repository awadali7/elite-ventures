Frequently Asked Question

What are the requirements to apply for a freelancer visa?

To apply for a freelancer visa, you will need, A passport copy, Photograph, Completed application form, Attested Educational Certificate, and Payment for the first year of work permit.

Can I apply for a freelancer visa without being in UAE?

Yes you can apply for a freelancer visa while Outside of the UAE. You however require to enter the UAE within 60 Days of the visa Issuance to complete the procedure to have it stamped in your passport. Failure to enter within 60 days may results in a forfeiting of the Visa, and the application will need to be reapplied bearing the entire cost once again.

I have lost my job and my company has started my visa cancellation; can I get a freelancer visa?

If your visa is currently, in the process of canceling, you will be required to provide us with your visa cancellation paper supported by the necessary documents in order to start your process of obtaining a freelancing visa with us.

If I currently have a full-time job, can I apply for a freelance permit only?

Yes Definitely. In order to carry out the process, you will be required to provide an NOC from your sponsor and/or employer.
