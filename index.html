Frequently Asked Question

What does VESA stand for?

VESA stands for Video Electronics Standards Association.

What is the purpose of VESA?

VESA is responsible for creating and promoting industry standards for video and display technologies, ensuring compatibility between devices and enabling features like plug-and-play and VESA mounting.

What is VESA mounting?

VESA mounting refers to a standardized pattern of mounting holes on the back of displays and monitors. This allows them to be easily attached to VESA-compatible monitor arms, stands, or wall mounts for flexible positioning and improved ergonomics.
